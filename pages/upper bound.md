alias:: 上界, bounded above

- # definition
	- Suppose $S$ is an [[ordered set]], and $E\subset S$. If there exists a $\beta\in S$ such that $x\le\beta$ for every $x\in E$, we say that $E$ is [[bounded above]], and call $\beta$ an [[upper bound]] of $E$.

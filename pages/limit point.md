alias:: limit points

- # Definition
  Let $X$ be a [[metric space]], [[point]] $p, q\in X, E\subset X$,
  A [[point]] $p$ is a [[limit point]] of the [[set]] $E$ if every [[neighborhood]] of $p$ contains a point $q\ne p$ such that $q\in E$.
	- >That is:
	  $$\forall N_r(p)\cap E\ne \emptyset\ \mathrm{or}\ \{p\}$$

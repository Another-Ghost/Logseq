- # Definition
  $$e=\sum_{n=0}^\infty\frac{1}{n!}$$
  Here $n! = 1 · 2 · 3 · · · n$ if $n\geq 1$, and $0! = 1$.
- # Theorem
	- logseq.order-list-type:: number
	  id:: 647a0831-3568-4c89-943e-f53086620cab
	  $$\lim_{n\to\infty}\left(1+\frac{1}{n}\right)^n=e$$
	- $e$ is [[irrational]].

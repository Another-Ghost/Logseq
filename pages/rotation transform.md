- ## 2D [[rotation matrix]]
	- The [[matrix]] $R_z(\theta)$ that performs a [[rotation]] through the *angle* $\theta$ about the $z$ [[axis]] is given by
	  $$
	  \mathbf{R}_{z}(\theta)=\begin{pmatrix}\cos\theta&-\sin\theta&0\\\sin\theta&\cos\theta&0\\0&0&1\end{pmatrix}
	  $$
